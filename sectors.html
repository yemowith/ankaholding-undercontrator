Sectors

Anka Holding operates across a wide range of sectors:


Construction: As pioneers of modern urbanization and infrastructure, we develop projects in the construction sector. With our quality design and engineering solutions, we make cities more livable.


Energy: We work across a wide range of energy solutions, from renewable energy sources to traditional energy. With eco-friendly and efficient energy production, we address the energy needs of the future from today.


Banking: By offering innovative products and solutions in the financial services sector, we provide the most suitable support to meet our clients' needs. We are building a reliable and dynamic structure in the banking industry.


Logistics: Our global and local logistics operations make a difference in supply chain management. With technology-driven solutions, we optimize transportation and storage processes.


Defense Industry: We develop high-tech products and services in the defense industry, meeting security needs. With our strong infrastructure and innovation-focused work, we are one of the leading names in the sector.


Tourism: By offering innovative services in the tourism sector, we provide our guests with unique experiences. With accommodation, travel, and event solutions, we stand out both locally and internationally.


Mining: We work on the efficient and environmentally-friendly extraction and processing of natural resources. With our expertise in the mining sector, we contribute to economic development.


Food, Agriculture, and Livestock: By providing sustainable solutions in the food, agriculture, and livestock sectors, we contribute to society's basic needs. With our innovative approaches, we offer productive and high-quality solutions.


Chemical: In the chemical sector, we produce quality products and solutions to meet the needs of both industry and daily life. We make a difference with our eco-friendly production processes.


National and International Trade: As a strong business partner in global markets, we provide reliable solutions in national and international trade. With our trade activities across various sectors, we add value to our business partners.

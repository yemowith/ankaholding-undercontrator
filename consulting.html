Consulting

At Anka Holding, we provide consultancy services in all sectors we operate, offering strategic guidance to our business partners and customers. With our expert teams and innovative approach, we develop solution-oriented business models.


Construction Consultancy: By providing the best solutions in the design, planning, and implementation phases of projects, we make construction processes more efficient and sustainable. Our project management, cost control, and quality inspection services ensure that our clients achieve their goals.


Energy Consultancy: We offer comprehensive consultancy services in renewable energy projects, energy efficiency, and eco-friendly solutions. By providing strategic guidance in energy investments, we combine environmental and economic benefits.


Banking Consultancy: Our expert team produces strategic solutions to optimize your financial processes and evaluate new market opportunities. We support the banking sector with digitalization, risk management, and improving customer experience.


Logistics Consultancy: We provide consultancy services in areas such as supply chain management, international transportation, and warehouse optimization, making your logistics processes more effective. Our technology-driven solutions accelerate your business processes.


Defense Industry Consultancy: We provide strategic guidance and technical support in high-tech projects, ensuring the successful completion of your defense industry projects. We offer expert consultancy services in R&D, production, and integration processes.


Tourism Consultancy: We provide guidance in business development, enhancing customer experience, and creating marketing strategies in the tourism sector. We provide tailored solutions for your accommodation and travel industry projects.


Mining Consultancy: We offer strategic planning and process optimization services for efficient extraction and processing of resources. With consultancy solutions focused on environmental sustainability and economic efficiency, we add value to the industry.


Food, Agriculture, and Livestock Consultancy: We provide consultancy in areas such as developing efficiency-enhancing projects in agriculture and livestock, raising quality standards in food production, and applying innovative agricultural technologies.


Chemical Consultancy: We offer guidance on process optimization, innovative product development, and environmental compliance in the chemical sector. We create solutions to make your business processes more efficient.


National and International Trade Consultancy: With our expertise in global trade networks, we provide strategic support for your national and international business processes. We offer guidance in market entry strategies, risk management, and trade agreements, taking your business to the next level.


Expert Staff and Technology-Focused Approach

Anka Holding combines its expert teams and innovative technologies in its consultancy services in every sector, providing solution-oriented services to its clients. We develop strategies tailored to the needs of your business and stand by you to make a difference in your industry.

Privacy Policy

At Anka Holding, we are highly sensitive about the security and privacy of your personal data. This Privacy Policy is prepared to explain how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. By using our website, you are declaring that you accept this privacy policy.


Data We Collect

We may collect various personal information through our website. These details are collected to provide better service to our users and are used only for the specified purposes. The data we collect may include: Name, surname, email address, phone number Website usage information (IP address, browser type, pages visited, etc.) Transaction history and customer requests, etc. We may use the collected personal data for the following purposes: Responding to user requests, Communicating and improving services, Marketing activities and promotions, Improving website performance, Fulfilling legal obligations and contractual requirements. Please contact us for more information about the data we collect.


Data Sharing

Anka Holding may share your personal data with third parties only when legally required or with your explicit consent. We only share your data with our partners and service providers under confidentiality agreements to ensure data security.


Data Security

The security of your personal data is a top priority for us. We use modern security technologies and procedures to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, please note that data transmissions over the internet may not be completely secure.


Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance the user experience. Cookies are small files that temporarily store user information while navigating the website. You can learn more about cookies and adjust your preferences using your browser settings.


Data Access and Correction

If you wish to view, correct, or delete your personal data, you can contact us. Your data access request will be evaluated within legal frameworks and we will respond as soon as possible.


Data Retention Period

Your personal data will only be retained for as long as required by legal obligations or within the scope of our business relationship. Once the period expires, your data will be securely deleted.


Changes and Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will announce any policy changes by posting them on our website.


Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding our privacy policy, you can contact us. Anka Holding respects the privacy of its users and takes all necessary measures to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy.

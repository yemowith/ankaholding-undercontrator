Corporate

Anka Holding is a leader that makes a difference in the business world with its broad vision and strong values. Operating in many sectors such as construction, energy, logistics, banking, defense industry, tourism, mining, chemicals, food, agriculture, and livestock, it adds value to society and business partners.


Since its establishment, Anka Holding has reached a prestigious position in global markets by acting with innovative approaches and high-quality standards. With its subsidiaries and brands, it implements projects that expand industry boundaries while offering modern solutions to its business partners.


Anka Holding aims to create social value through its technology-driven work and sustainability approach. Our holding continues to be a trusted and successful business partner for its stakeholders, advancing with strategies that make a difference in every sector.


With the strength derived from our values, we are signing innovative and effective projects that meet the needs of not only today but also tomorrow. By prioritizing quality, trust, and sustainability in our work, we make meaningful contributions for both individuals and communities.

Our Team

Behind the success of Anka Holding is a team of experts and experienced professionals. Each of our professionals, leaders in their fields, works with an innovative and solution-oriented approach to achieve common goals.


Values

Anka Holding aims to contribute not only to the business world but also to society. By carrying out social responsibility projects in areas such as education, environment, technology, and innovation, we are working for a more livable world.

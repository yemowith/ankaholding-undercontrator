Projects

At Anka Holding, we make a difference with innovative and effective projects in all the sectors we operate. Our projects span a wide range of industries, including construction, energy, logistics, banking, defense industry, tourism, mining, food, agriculture, and livestock, aiming to add value to society and provide sustainable solutions.


In each project, we bring together strategy and creative ideas with our expert teams to carry out quality, results-oriented work. The projects we build by utilizing innovative technologies are designed not only to meet today's needs but also with the mission of being leaders in their industries.


While working to support our clients in achieving their goals, we also prioritize our environmental and social responsibilities. Anka Holding continues to add value to both its business partners and the society it is part of by delivering successful projects on both national and international scales.


Anka Holding makes a difference in the business world with its expertise in various sectors and innovative approach.
